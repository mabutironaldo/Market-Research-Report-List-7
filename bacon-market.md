<p><p>Bacon market types can be categorized into three main segments: dry cured, immersion cured, and others. </p><p>Dry cured bacon is crafted through a process of rubbing salt and spices onto the meat, allowing it to cure naturally over time, resulting in a rich flavor. Immersion cured bacon involves submerging the meat in a brine solution, which enhances moisture and tenderness. The "others" category includes alternative curing methods, such as smoking or utilizing innovative flavor infusions, appealing to diverse consumer preferences and culinary trends.</p></p>
