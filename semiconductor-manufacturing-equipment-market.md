<p><p>The semiconductor manufacturing equipment market is divided into two primary segments: front-end equipment and back-end equipment. Front-end equipment encompasses tools used during the initial phases of semiconductor fabrication, including wafer fabrication, photolithography, and etching. In contrast, back-end equipment involves processes that occur after wafer production, such as assembly, packaging, and testing of the final semiconductor devices. Together, these segments are essential for the complete semiconductor manufacturing process, impacting performance, yield, and overall production efficiency.</p></p>
