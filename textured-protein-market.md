<p><ul><li>Protein Concentrate</li><li>Protein Isolate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The textured protein market primarily consists of two main types: protein concentrates and protein isolates. Protein concentrates contain a higher percentage of protein, typically 60-80%, while retaining some carbohydrates and fats. They are commonly used in meat alternatives and processed foods for added protein content. On the other hand, protein isolates have a protein content of 90% or more, with minimal fats and carbohydrates, making them ideal for sports nutrition and dietary supplements. Both types cater to the growing demand for plant-based protein solutions.</p></p>
