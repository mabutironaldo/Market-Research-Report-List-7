Electric heaters are convenient and eco-friendly, requiring only an electrical source, suitable for smaller settings. Natural gas heaters provide a continuous heat supply and are cost-effective for larger venues, often connected to existing gas lines. Each type caters to different operational needs and environmental considerations for outdoor heating solutions.</p></p>
