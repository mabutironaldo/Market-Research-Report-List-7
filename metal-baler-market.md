<p><ul><li>Manual</li><li>PLC Automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metal baler market can be categorized into manual and PLC automatic types. Manual balers require operators to initiate and control the baling process, offering a lower initial investment but demanding more labor and time. In contrast, PLC automatic balers utilize programmable logic controllers for automated operation, enhancing efficiency and consistency while minimizing labor costs. As industries focus on optimizing production, the demand for PLC automatic balers is rising, driven by their ability to streamline operations and ensure high-quality bales.</p></p>
