<p><p>The automotive sunroof insulation film market can be categorized into two main types: solar control and convection control. Solar control films are designed to reduce solar heat gain and glare from sunlight, enhancing cabin comfort and minimizing the need for air conditioning. Convection control films, on the other hand, focus on preventing heat loss by insulating against outside temperatures, thereby maintaining a stable interior climate. Together, these films improve thermal efficiency, contributing to enhanced passenger comfort and vehicle energy efficiency.</p></p>
