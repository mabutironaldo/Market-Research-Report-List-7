<p><p>The 3D laser measure system market is divided into two main types: non-contact and contact measurement methods. Non-contact systems use laser technology to measure distances and dimensions from a safe distance, offering quick and accurate data collection without physical interaction. In contrast, contact measurement systems involve direct physical touch with the object being measured, often providing high precision in industrial applications. Both types cater to diverse industries, enhancing measurement accuracy and efficiency in various tasks like construction, manufacturing, and surveying.</p></p>
