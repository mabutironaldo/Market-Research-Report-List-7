<p><ul><li>Internal Circulation Ballscrew</li><li>External Circulation Ballscrew</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Rotary Ball Screws market is divided into two main types: Internal Circulation Ballscrews and External Circulation Ballscrews. Internal Circulation Ballscrews feature a design where the balls circulate within the nut, providing compactness and efficiency, suitable for space-constrained applications. In contrast, External Circulation Ballscrews have a configuration where the balls return outside the nut, allowing for higher load capacities and smoother operation. Both types are essential for precise linear motion in various industries, including aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing.</p></p>
