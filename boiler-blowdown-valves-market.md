<p><p>The boiler blowdown valves market is segmented by blowdown type into intermittent and continuous. Intermittent blowdown valves are used for periodic discharge of water to maintain steam quality and reduce impurities, while continuous blowdown valves provide a consistent flow to control concentration levels in the boiler. Product types are classified as top or bottom blowdown valves, where top valves remove surface water for lower solids and impurities, and bottom valves discharge sediment and sludge accumulated at the bottom of the boiler.</p></p>
