<p><p>The electrical switchgear market is categorized into three main types: high voltage, medium voltage, and low voltage switchgear. High voltage switchgear operates at voltages above 36 kV, primarily used in transmission networks. Medium voltage switchgear, ranging from 1 kV to 36 kV, is essential for industrial and commercial applications. Low voltage switchgear, operating up to 1 kV, is used in residential and light commercial settings. Each type plays a crucial role in power distribution, protection, and control within electrical systems.</p></p>
