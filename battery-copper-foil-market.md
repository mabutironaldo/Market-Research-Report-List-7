<p><p>The battery copper foil market comprises various types based on thickness, essential for different applications in battery production. Very thin copper foil (≤6μm) is used for ultra-lightweight batteries, while ultra-thin (6-12μm) and thin foils (12-18μm) serve in advanced lithium-ion cells for electric vehicles. Common copper foil (18-70μm) is widely employed in standard batteries, while thick copper foil (>70μm) is utilized in applications requiring enhanced conductivity and robustness, catering to emerging energy storage technologies.</p></p>
