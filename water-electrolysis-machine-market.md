<p><p>The water electrolysis machine market is primarily divided into three types: Proton Exchange Membrane (PEM) electrolysis, Alkaline water electrolysis, and Other technologies. PEM electrolysis involves a solid polymer electrolyte, offering fast response times and high efficiency, suitable for dynamic applications. Alkaline water electrolysis uses a liquid electrolyte solution, providing cost-effective solutions with proven technology for large-scale hydrogen production. The "Others" category includes emerging technologies, such as solid oxide electrolysis, which explore innovative methods for hydrogen generation and energy conversion.</p></p>
