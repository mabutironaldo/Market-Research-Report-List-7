<p><p>Moisture barrier fabric for law enforcement and firefighting is divided into breathable and non-breathable types. Breathable fabrics allow moisture vapor to escape while preventing water and contaminants from entering, ensuring comfort and reducing heat stress during intense activities. Non-breathable fabrics, on the other hand, provide a more rigid barrier against liquids but can lead to discomfort and overheating, as they trap moisture inside. Both types serve critical roles in protective gear, balancing safety and performance based on operational needs.</p></p>
