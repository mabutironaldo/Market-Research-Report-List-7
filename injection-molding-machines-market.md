<p><p>The injection molding machines market is primarily segmented into mechanical and hydraulic types. Mechanical injection molding machines utilize mechanical drives for high-speed production, offering greater precision and energy efficiency. They are ideal for smaller, intricate parts. In contrast, hydraulic injection molding machines employ hydraulic systems, providing powerful clamping force and versatility for larger components. Although generally slower and less energy-efficient, they are suited for heavy-duty applications. Both types cater to diverse manufacturing needs across multiple industries.</p></p>
