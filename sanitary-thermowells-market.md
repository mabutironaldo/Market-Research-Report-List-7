<p><p>The sanitary thermowells market includes several types, each designed for specific applications. Tapered thermowells feature a gradually narrowing design that minimizes blockage and enhances flow, making them suitable for viscous fluids. Straight thermowells maintain a uniform diameter, providing stability and ease of installation in standard applications. Stepped thermowells combine features of both tapered and straight designs, optimizing temperature measurement and reducing fluid turbulence. These variations cater to diverse industrial needs, ensuring accurate temperature sensing in sanitary environments.</p></p>
