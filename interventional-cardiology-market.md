<p><p>The interventional cardiology market comprises various devices essential for minimally invasive heart procedures. Cardiology catheters are utilized for diagnostic and therapeutic interventions. Guidewires assist in navigating vascular pathways during procedures. Balloon catheters dilate narrowed arteries to improve blood flow. Coronary stents provide structural support to keep arteries open post-intervention. Fractional flow reserve measures blood flow and pressure in coronary arteries to assess blockages, while intravascular ultrasound offers real-time imaging for better decision-making during interventions.</p></p>
