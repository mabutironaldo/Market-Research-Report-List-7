<p><p>The tire balance market primarily consists of two types: clip-on and adhesive weights. Clip-on weights attach directly to the rim and are ideal for steel wheels, providing easy installation and adjustment. Adhesive weights, on the other hand, adhere to the inner side of the wheel rim, offering a cleaner aesthetic and reduced airflow disruption. Both types are essential for ensuring proper tire balance, enhancing vehicle performance, and ensuring safety by minimizing vibrations and uneven tire wear.</p></p>
