<p><p>The CMP Diamond Disks market comprises two main types: Conventional CMP Diamond Pad Disks and CVD Diamond CMP Pad Disks. Conventional CMP pads utilize sintered diamond particles for efficient polishing and are suitable for a variety of materials. In contrast, CVD (Chemical Vapor Deposition) diamond pads are engineered through a process that allows for superior hardness and thermal stability, enhancing productivity and finish quality. Both types serve pivotal roles in semiconductor and advanced material manufacturing, catering to specific application needs.</p></p>
