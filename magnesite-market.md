<p><p>The magnesite market is categorized into two main types: phanerocrystalline and cryptocrystalline magnesite. Phanerocrystalline magnesite consists of large crystal formations, making it suitable for high-quality refractory applications due to its superior thermal stability. In contrast, cryptocrystalline magnesite features fine-grained structures, often used in ceramics and chemical applications. Both types cater to distinct industrial needs, with demand driven by sectors like metallurgy, construction, and environmental applications, contributing to overall market dynamics.</p></p>
