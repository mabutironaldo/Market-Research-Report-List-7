<p><p>Cow cubicles are a key component in dairy farming, providing comfortable resting spaces for cattle. There are two main types: flexible and wall-mounted. Flexible cubicles offer adjustable partitions, allowing customization based on cow size and milk production needs. This adaptability enhances cow comfort and overall farm efficiency. Wall-mounted cubicles are fixed to the barn structure, providing a stable and space-efficient option. They maximize barn space and ensure a safe, constrained area for cows, promoting better lying behavior and health.</p></p>
