<p><p>Smart lamp switches come in various types primarily differentiated by their connectivity options: Wi-Fi and Bluetooth. Wi-Fi smart switches can connect directly to a home network, allowing for remote control via smartphone apps and integration with smart home systems. They typically offer wider range and can be controlled from anywhere with internet access. In contrast, Bluetooth smart switches operate within a limited range, typically connecting directly to devices like smartphones. They are easier to install but may lack some advanced features and remote access capabilities.</p></p>
