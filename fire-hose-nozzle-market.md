<p><p>The fire hose nozzle market comprises two primary types: low pressure nozzles and high pressure nozzles. Low pressure nozzles are designed for applications requiring greater flow rates at lower forces, providing a wide spray pattern ideal for cooling and soaking fires. In contrast, high pressure nozzles produce a focused stream with significant impact force, suitable for penetrating deep into fires or reaching distant targets. Each type serves distinct operational needs, enhancing firefighting effectiveness in various scenarios.</p></p>
