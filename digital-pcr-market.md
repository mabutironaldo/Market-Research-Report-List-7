Microreactor/Orifice Plate Digital PCR utilizes small reaction chambers for high-throughput analysis. Microfluidic Chip Digital PCR integrates multiple processes on a chip, enabling efficient sample handling and reduced reagent use. Droplet Digital PCR partitions samples into droplets for independent amplification, resulting in enhanced sensitivity and accuracy. Each type offers unique advantages, catering to diverse research and clinical applications in genomics and molecular biology.</p></p>
