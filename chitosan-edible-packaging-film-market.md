<p><p>Chitosan edible packaging films are categorized into three crystal forms: α, β, and γ. The α crystal form exhibits high solubility and is ideal for moisture-sensitive applications. The β crystal form offers enhanced mechanical strength and barrier properties, making it suitable for various food packaging needs. The γ crystal form is less common but can provide unique properties like improved antimicrobial activity. These different forms allow for tailored applications in food preservation, enhancing shelf life while remaining biodegradable and safe for consumption.</p></p>
