<p><p>The Metric O-Rings market is divided into two main types: static seals and dynamic seals. Static seals are used in applications where the O-Ring does not experience motion, effectively preventing leaks in stationary assemblies. Dynamic seals, on the other hand, are designed for applications involving relative movement, accommodating motion while maintaining a reliable seal. Both types are critical in various industries, ensuring the integrity of systems by preventing fluid or gas leaks in diverse environments and machinery.</p></p>
