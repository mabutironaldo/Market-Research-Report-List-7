<p><p>The automotive dynamometer market is primarily divided into two types: engine dynamometers and chassis dynamometers. Engine dynamometers test individual engine performance in controlled conditions, allowing evaluation of parameters like power output and torque. In contrast, chassis dynamometers assess the entire vehicle's performance, recording how it behaves under real-world conditions while driving. Both types are essential for manufacturers and tuners to optimize engine performance, improve fuel efficiency, and ensure compliance with emissions regulations.</p></p>
