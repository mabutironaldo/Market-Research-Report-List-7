<p><p>The PVC membranes market is divided into two primary types: plasticized and unplasticized PVC membranes. Plasticized PVC membranes contain added plasticizers, making them more flexible and suitable for applications requiring adaptability, such as roofing and waterproofing. Unplasticized PVC membranes, on the other hand, lack these additives, resulting in a more rigid structure that is ideal for applications demanding durability and resistance to environmental factors, such as pipelines and construction materials. Each type serves distinct needs within various industries.</p></p>
