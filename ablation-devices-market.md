<p><ul><li>Thermal Ablation</li><li>Non-Thermal Ablation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ablation devices market is segmented into thermal and non-thermal ablation types. Thermal ablation utilizes heat to destroy targeted tissues, often employing methods like radiofrequency or laser energy, commonly used in procedures like tumor removal or cardiac ablation. Non-thermal ablation, on the other hand, employs mechanisms such as cryoablation or microwave energy, targeting tissues without relying on heat. Each type offers distinct advantages based on treatment goals, patient conditions, and procedural requirements, contributing to the diverse landscape of the market.</p></p>
