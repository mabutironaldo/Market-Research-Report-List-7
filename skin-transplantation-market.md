<p><strong>The Skin Transplantation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Split-Thickness</li><li>Full-Thickness</li><li>Composite Graft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The skin transplantation market includes various types of grafts: split-thickness, full-thickness, and composite grafts. Split-thickness grafts involve only the epidermis and a part of the dermis, promoting faster healing and less scarring, making them suitable for larger wounds. Full-thickness grafts encompass the entire dermis and epidermis, providing better cosmetic results but requiring more care during healing. Composite grafts combine skin with underlying tissues, addressing complex defects and enhancing functionality, particularly in reconstructive surgeries. Each type serves distinct clinical needs in skin restoration.</p></p>
