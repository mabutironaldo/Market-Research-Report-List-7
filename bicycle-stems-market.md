<p><p>The bicycle stems market encompasses various types designed for different riding preferences and bike styles. Threadless stems, popular for modern bikes, provide a strong and adjustable connection to the handlebars without a quill system. Adjustable stems offer versatility, allowing riders to change the height and angle for a custom fit. Quill stems, commonly found in older bicycles, feature a simple design that fits into the bike's frame but limits adjustability. Each type caters to distinct performance and comfort needs.</p></p>
