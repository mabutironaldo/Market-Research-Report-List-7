<p><strong>The 3D Printer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FDM（Fused Deposition Model）</li><li>SLA（Stereolithography）</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D printer market consists of various technologies, primarily FDM (Fused Deposition Modeling) and SLA (Stereolithography). FDM printers extrude thermoplastic filament layer by layer, making them popular for prototyping and affordable prints. SLA uses a laser to cure liquid resin into solid layers, resulting in high-resolution and detailed outputs, ideal for intricate designs and professional applications. Both technologies cater to different user needs, with FDM being more accessible for hobbyists and SLA favored in industries requiring precision.</p></p>
