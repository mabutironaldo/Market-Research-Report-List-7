<p><p>The PTFE composite film market features two primary types: hydrophobic and hydrophilic PTFE composite films. Hydrophobic PTFE composite films are designed to repel water, making them suitable for applications requiring moisture resistance and non-stick properties, commonly used in protective coatings and seals. In contrast, hydrophilic PTFE composite films absorb water, enhancing adhesion and improving filtration capabilities, making them ideal for medical and filtration applications. Both types leverage the unique properties of PTFE to serve diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
