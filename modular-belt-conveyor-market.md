<p><p>The modular belt conveyor market comprises two main types: open belt conveyors and enclosed belt conveyors. Open belt conveyors are designed for easy access and maintenance, commonly used in environments where visibility and regular checks are necessary. In contrast, enclosed belt conveyors provide protection against external factors like dust, moisture, and contaminants, making them ideal for sensitive or hazardous applications. Both types enhance material handling efficiency, but their selection depends on specific operational needs and environmental conditions.</p></p>
