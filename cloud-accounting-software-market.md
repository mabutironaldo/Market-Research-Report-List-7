<p><p>Cloud accounting software is categorized into three main types: browser-based, SaaS, and Application Service Providers (ASPs). Browser-based solutions are accessed directly through web browsers, offering flexibility and ease of use. SaaS (Software as a Service) provides a subscription model where users access the software over the internet, ensuring regular updates and support. ASPs offer a more traditional model by hosting applications on remote servers, allowing users to access accounting software without local installation, typically on a per-user or subscription basis.</p></p>
