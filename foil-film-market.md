<p><p>Foil film market segments are categorized by thickness, specifically 12μm, 16μm, 18μm, and 20μm variants. The 12μm foil is typically lightweight and often used for packaging snacks and light products. The 16μm thickness provides enhanced durability, suitable for mid-range packaging solutions. The 18μm variant offers a balance of strength and flexibility, ideal for versatile applications. Finally, the 20μm film is the thickest, providing superior barrier properties and is favored for heavy-duty packaging requirements, ensuring product freshness and security.</p></p>
